It wasn't until recently that I became interested in Web Developement. Spending the last 6+ years of my life as a "Digital Nomad", I spent my days building e-Commerce businesses from the ground up and selling them after a few succesful years. The Philippines was my home, and I was happy. But I grew tired of the "same old, same old", and longed for a new challenge. "How are these websites I'm running even built?" I wondered. I did a bit of Googling (a must-have skill in the world of Web Developement, so I'm told), and learned about HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Not having a clue about what any of these languages were, I was intrigued. I signed up for Codecademy and Free Code Camp, among others (and also spent dozens of hours reading Stack Overflow Q&A's). In a moment's notice, I had become completely thrown into the deep, dark rabbit hole of Web Developement - and I knew that there was no going back.

Entirely Self-Taught

Everything I know about coding was learned through free (or very low-cost) educational resources on the internet.

E-commerce Entrepreneur

I formerly ran & sold multiple six-figure e-Commerce businesses, where I stared at webpages 8 hours a day and wondered how to build them myself.

Driven To Learn

I pride myself on being a self-starter and go-getter, and hope to someday be an asset to your awesome team.
